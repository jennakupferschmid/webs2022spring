About Me

Hi! My name is Jenna Kupferschmid and I am currently a sophomore at the University of Pittsburgh majoring in communications with certificates in public and professional writing and digital media. At the University I have become proficient in Canva, Photoshop, Audacity Excel, Powerpoint, Github, Atom, and Word.

Currently, I am a tour guide within the Pitt Pathfinders organization at the University. Within the Pathfinder organization I was responsible for leading the summer social media accounts and successfully worked on deadline-driven projects with a small group of colleagues in order to produce marketing content for the organization. Additionally, I consistently lead groups throughout campus and facilitate communication between our officer team and prospective students. I am also on the General Board of my sorority, Alpha Epsilon Phi, where I help organize events for the 100+ person chapter.

I plan to take all my past experiences to gain success in the marketing and public relations fields in the future.
